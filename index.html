Dhoolpet

Dhoolpet is part of the Old City of Hyderabad India, partly inhabited by people who migrated from Uttar Pradesh during the Nizam rule. The area is well-known for illegal bootlegging.The Jummerat Bazaar (Thursday Market) is a flea market held every Thursday in Dhoolpet.

Activities In Dhoolpet

# Legal Activities #

1.Majarity Of People What They Are Do For Their Living

They Work in Lord ganesha Idol making and Durga matha Idol making for year lo long, prepare them and sell them in the month of september . People from many places and distances come purchasing idols as dhoolpet has its own reputation making fantastic Idols of different verities , models , special design and its finishing . They got a Uniqueness in the idols which are superior to idols made all over india. People from andhra pradesh , Bangalore, mumbai , maharashtra and different towns and cities come to purchase the idols from dhoolpet since years.

2.Daily Wagers

Also majority of people work as daily wagers for many construction companies. Doing all the labour work and hands behind the making.

3.Statue Makers

Statues of any kind are made here by artists of dhoolpet, and do differentkind Statues like Ganesha, Durgadevi etc...

4.Buggies Spply For Weddings

Also at dhoolpet entrance we come to see many horses and buggies . Which is dhoolpet also popular for , weddings require buggies from all over HYDERABAD people come to dhoolpet to hire buggies. There is the only major place they can find buggies in larger numbers.

5. Welding And Forging Works

Beside the roads of dhoolpet . Can find lot welding and forging shops here and there . People work on such kind of works are also more also the work can be get done at cheapest price.

# Illegal Activities #

1.Illegal Bootlegging

Since many years their live a small group of people who prepare and sell Illegal alcohol for their living , locally called as saara or gudumba which is considered a major crime according to indian judiciary.

2.Manja Selling

Earlier using manja's for kite flying was quiet common during sankranthi festival. But in the recent years government taken an action considering accident cases of using manja and found hazardous for the Economy. Banned the use and making of manja's in the country. Since no one is allowed to manufacture or Sell . There is no place to buy manja's. Considering the demand for manja in people dhoolpet managed to sell manja's illegally during the festival . People still continue to buy manja from dhoolpet even in restrictions and despite police checkings they manage to get manja from dhoolpet.

3.Marijuna Selling

Since decades dhoolpet is quite famous for its ganja selling and marijuana products like chocolates and stuff . Peddlers manage to bring marijuana to dhoolpet from villages or from other states and villages. Sell the stuff to consumer's . Consumers involve students, youth , adults, daily wage workers, also movie actors from tollywood etc. Since the marijuana consumption in the country has increased these days . GOVERNMENT taken an initiative to remove or abolish marijuana in the state . Peddlers from dhoolpet were taken into custody . Filed cases against the accused for illegal activities and imprisonment as a punishment. Also all the parts of state are also taken the same initiative and locked the substance transport since marijuana selling and consumption dropped to zero . That's the Good news to the society and also great initiative by the state government.

4.Jummerat Bazaar

Jummerat bazaar beside dhoolpet also called as chor bazaar of Hyderabad. Every Thursday a market is held along the footpath of roads of jummerat bazaar. Where different kinds and variety of items related to home needs, dresses, kitchen needs, mobiles, laptops, TVs, cycles, tyres,gym items, watches,hardware items , a to z items are sold by vendor's at cheap price. These are all the items sold to vendors by thieves for soo small amount of cash . So vendors sell these items for a cheap price . Also the jummerat bazaar roads on Monday are crowded by huge amount of buyers .
